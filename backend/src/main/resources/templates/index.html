Empowering Agriculture

Driving Digital Agri-Innovation

The AI-Powered Smart Agriculture & Livestock Disease Diagnosis Portal is a comprehensive government-backed digital ecosystem. Built on secure Java microservices and optimized Python deep-learning engines, it allows real-time diagnostics directly from the field.

Whether dealing with crop fungi or cattle viral pathogens, the platform gives instant step-by-step guidance on pesticide applications, organic treatments, and direct connectivity to local government veterinary stations.

Portal Capabilities

Explore the deep features offered to the farming community and veterinary officers.

Plant Pathology

Upload crop foliage images to detect diseases like late blight or rust. Receive severity calculations and prescription advice instantly.

Livestock Health

Run image diagnostics for animal pathogens (FMD, Lumpy Skin, Mastitis). Recieve quarantine isolation advice and nearby vet office referral.

Soil Analytics

Input soil N-P-K parameters and moisture variables. The ML system will suggest suitable crops and appropriate fertilizer quantities.

Yield Regressor

Predict yield output per hectare depending on meteorological statistics, soil composition, and fertilizer tonnage.

Market Predictions

Leverage time-series commodity price prediction models. Make optimal decisions on when and where to list crops for maximum profit.

Tele-Medicine

Directly book slot consultations with certified veterinary officers. Track diagnostic summaries, review reports, and export clinical PDFs.
